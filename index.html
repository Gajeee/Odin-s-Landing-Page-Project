Story

A psychological horror premise—kept vague on purpose. Your imagination is the sharpest blade.

Act I — The Return

You arrive with a name you don’t trust, and a letter you don’t remember writing. The building recognizes you anyway.

Act II — The Choir

Each corridor hums. Each hymn is a map. Each verse asks you to choose: truth, or peace.

Act III — The Mirror

A confession waits behind stained glass. But the face in the reflection will not match the one you brought.
